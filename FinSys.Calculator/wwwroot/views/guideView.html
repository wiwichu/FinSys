Cash Flows



Cash Flow Grid

  • Scheduled Date - The date the payment was scheduled.
  • Adjusted Date - Scheduled date after any non-business day adjustment. This is the date which will actually be used when determining the present value.
  • Amount - amount of the payment.
  • Present Value - Value of the amount after applying the discount rate.
  • Discount Rate - Rate by which the amount is discounted to arrive at the present value.
